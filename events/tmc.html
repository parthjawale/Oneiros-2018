OCTAVES

“A song can be more than words and music. When sung with soul, a song carries you to another world, to a place where no matter how much pain you feel, you are never alone.” Oneiros 18 presents to you our very own Western Solo Singing Competition, where you can let your songs and voice transcend all the boundaries. So come on and sing the song to make the whole world sing.

Venue

Prelims - MOOT Court Finals - TMA PAI Auditorium

Timings

Prelims – 9 a.m. to 11 a.m. Day 3 Finals – 3 p.m. to 5 p.m. Day 3

Price

X for Y members

Co-ordinators

ChinmayGaur :- 8433055358 chinmay268ds@gmail.com

LakshyaGarg :- 9871697303 lakshyagarg57@gmail.com

Rules

* The competition is split into 2 rounds.

Preliminary Round

1. Time Limit is 1 minutes & 30 seconds.
2. No accompanist or karaoke is allowed.
3. If time limit is exceeded, negative marks would be awarded accordingly.
4. Reading the lyrics is not allowed.
5. Decision of the judges is final and binding.

Final Round

1. Time Limit is 6 minutes (Including Sound-Setup).
2. Maximum of 2 Accompanists or a karaoke is allowed.
3. If time limit is exceeded, negative marks would be awarded.
4. Reading the lyrics is not allowed.
5. Decision of the judges is final and binding.
6. The participants are required to be ready with the karaoke tracks in a pen drive or in mobile phones.
7. Only 1 keyboard will be provided to the participants.
8. Judging will be done completely on the basis of vocals only. (there are no marks for the instruments.)
9. The Organising Committee has the right to mend rules, disqualify entries or cancel the event prior to its initiation.

ENSEMBLE

“It’s more fun, if you are more than one!” Ensemble is a theme based group singing event; an opportunity to show how beautiful different voices can sound together. From baritones to falsettos, every voice counts and can sound magical together when blended in the right way. To all the musicians out there, come out with your rhythmic buddies, blend in all your unique voices and instruments to create magic! If you think you can change people for better with your voice and have friends who think the same, come together and unite your voices and skills, prepare an exquisite group song that will leave music-muggles and maestros awestruck alike.
