Sadharan ‘The General Quiz’

This quiz will deal with all the things we consider general in life like geography, politics, sports,etc. it will be focused more on our country India than any other country. Questions that can be asked can be on the basis like- who was the first president of india? , With how many countries does India share border with etc. Participants should focus more on geography, politics and unique things found in india etc.

Venue

Sharda Pai Auditorium

Timings

11 a.m to 1 p.m, Day 2

Co-ordinators

Rachit Singh :- 9871357049

Anil Kumar Nayak :- 896940650

Rules

1. There will be 4 rounds and five teams will qualify for the finals.
2. A team can have 1 or 2 individuals only.
3. Quizmaster’s decision is final
4. No phones are allowed during the quiz. Anyone found using their phone will be disqualified immediately.
