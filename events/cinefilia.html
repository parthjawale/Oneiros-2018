Ad-Mak
Advertisement Making

“I couldn’t choose my husband but I can choose my jewellery”-Tanishq “It Gives You Wiiiings!”-Red Bull Attracted by these funny taglines? Amazed by the commercials you see on your TV? Ever wondered how fun it could be making our own advertisements? Let’s give it a shot! Come up with the ideas to create your own commercial! Here comes AD- MAK which gives you a chance to show your creativity! Why not do it?

Venue

SMT. Sharda Pai Auditorium, Acedemic Block 2

Timing

Sunday 11:00am-1:00pm

Co-ordinators

RAVISHA KHURANA (8700130477/8529642691) ravishakhurana238@gmail.com

AKSHAT MEHROTRA (7275099881) akshatedu123@gmail.com

Rules

* Only team registrations are allowed. Each team must have a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 participants.
* The teams will get a product to sell via chits and products will be decided by the event organizers. * Preparation time given would be 15 minutes.
* The time limit is 2-5 minutes. Not obeying the time slot will result in the deduction of points.
* Language used can be Hindi, English or both.
* Points will be deducted for plagiarism.
* Abusive words or obscenity is not allowed and the teams would be disqualified for doing so.
* The suitable prop would be provided and proper use of prop will fetch the team extra points.
* Each team must come up with a catchphrase/pick-up line for the product alloted.
* The decision of judges would be final.
* The Organizing Committee can mend the rules and disqualify entries if the situation demands so.

Humor Us
Advertisement Making

Are you that guy who cheers up the group and makes everybody Go rolling on the floor laughing? Do you have the talent of making even the most serious of situations funny ? Do you think that your jokes are pun-tastic and you have the wit to tickle the funny bone? Is being observational and jesting your forte? Well, HUMOR-US is giving you the platform to showcase your talent. Also we have a huge surprise for you!!! For the winning contestant humor-us is going to be LEGEN…wait for it DARY, because the winner will get a direct entry to IIT BOMBAY’S: MOOD INDIGO- Asia’s largest cultural festival.

Venue

TMA PAI AUDITORIUM

Timing

ELIMINATIONS : 2 hours 30 mins
FINALS : 1 hour 30 mins

Co-ordinators

RAVISHA KHURANA (8700130477/8529642691) ravishakhurana238@gmail.com

AKSHAT MEHROTRA (7275099881) akshatedu123@gmail.com

Rules

Team size: Solo Judging Criteria: Content, Quality of humour, Stage presence, Audience engagement, Clarity of thought, Wackiness, USP(Unique Selling Point). Eliminations Eliminations are to be conducted on-ground during the fest, where the participants have to put on a show of upto 4 minutes in front of a live audience. After 3 minutes a single bell sound will be played. After 3 mins 30 secs double bell. At 4 mins the mic audio will be cut. Finals The participants who cleared the eliminations will be asked to prepare a sketch of maximum 5 minutes, to perform in front of a live audience. After 4 minutes a single bell sound will be played. After 4 mins 30 secs double bell. At 4 mins the mic audio will be cut. General Rules 1.Mono-acting, puppetry, musicals are not allowed though ventriloquism is allowed. Use of props is allowed, brought in by participant himself. 2.The organisers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage. 3.Both English and Hindi are allowed. No other language is allowed. 4.Decisions taken by the judges is absolute and binding. 5.Use of obscenity/profanity(at the discretion of judges) is not allowed and there should be no direct implications. 6.Any Form of music is not allowed during the performance. 7.Since the participants are called upon in random order, each is required to be present at least 5 minutes prior to the start. 8.Organisers will not be responsible if you are unable to show up on time and miss your shot.
