Steps VS Beats

For a long time, the battle for the best has been fought. For the first time ever, Oneiros in collaboration with Coreografia brings to you the Ultimate Clash of Stage Vs Street. We present to you, STEPS VS BEATS, a solo dance battle where you fight for those Bragging Rights... for your Form... for your Ground. So charmers and swaggers, STEP UP and DANCE IT OUT. Who do you want to take to victory?

Rules

1) The event is a solo all style dance battle event.
2) According to the dance form mentioned during registration, all contestants will be divided into two teams: Street and Stage.
3) All contestants should stick to genres and dance forms of their respective teams (Stage or Street). Adopting a dance form of the opposite teamâ€™s genre/dance form might result in disqualification.
4) No participants should indulge in any kind of physical or verbal abuse ( slogans on clothing, nicknames etc) at any instances (before, during or after the event) there maintaining the decorum of the event. In case of any sort of disturbance created by a participant, a warning will be given after which on further breaking of rules shall lead to disqualification.
5) No props are allowed.
6) The decision of the judges will be final and absolute. Any arguments regarding the results will not be entertained.

8) All battle and management related decisions will be in the hands of the organizing committee and reserve the right to modify or cancel the performance.
9) Registrations will be close 1 hour prior to event.
10) All participants are recommended to reach the venue 40 mins prior to the event.
11) All participants must carry third college/institutional/school IDs at all times.
