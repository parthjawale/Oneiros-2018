Rules

Round 1

This round will be a crossword round in which every team will have to complete the crossword with a given time limit. The crossword will be around facts from the world of literature and pop culture. The teams who fail to complete the crossword in the given time limit will get eliminated immediately. Teams will get points based on how fast they complete the task the number of questions they get right.

Round 2-5

The winner of the preceding rounds gets to choose a genre out of four options given to the team at the start of each round. Based on this genre teams will be given puzzles or miniature games which they will have to solve. Again, there will be a certain time limit given to complete the puzzle and the marking procedure will be the same as the 1 st round.

Round 6

Only five teams will go through for the last round, these teams will be the ones who have the highest points. These five teams will compete on a life-sized snake and ladders board. Where instead of rolling a dice, each team will proceed based on the answers they give.
Once the question is shown on the screen each team must choose the correct option out of the four within 30seconds and put it down on a chit and press the buzzer. The anchors will come and collect the chit. Once the 30seconds period has elapsed then the anchors will announce the answers of each team and the number of steps they are to take on the board. Just as the rules of snakes and ladders if one reaches the bottom of a ladder then they must climb up the ladder. But if you get bitten by a snake you come down the snake.
