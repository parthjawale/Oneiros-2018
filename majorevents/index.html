Rules
Preliminary Video Round
  • Duration: 3-4 min.
  • Teams have to send their respective videos to destival.outlook.com before 11 th October, 2018 (Wed), 11:59 PM.
  • In case the video size exceeds the mail limit, then a Google Drive Link of the video must be sent.
  • The following details are to be sent along with the Video:
  • Name of college
  • Name of team
  • Name and contact number of the team leader
  • List of names of team members in the video
  • List of names of team members coming for the final round
  • The members that are performing in the Video must also be a part of the final team.
  • Music tracks for preliminary and final round may be different but the routine performed in the prelims video should necessarily be a part of the Final Routine.
  • Teams can choose any form of dance and music for their performance (following the theme is not compulsory).
  • Competition is reserved only for current college students. (Professional dancers or students from other colleges are not allowed to represent a college)
  • Late entries will not be accepted under any circumstances.
  • Decisions of the Judges will be final and binding.
  • Disregard of any rule will not be tolerated and will lead to disqualification.
Final Round:
  • Duration: 8 to 10 min (10 min max.).
  • Team limit: 12 to 40 participants + 3 helpers (per team)
  • Minimum number of participants on stage at any instance during performance: 5 members
  • Maximum number of members on stage during performance: 25 members
  • Multiple Teams from a college are allowed.
  • Misbehavior or indecency with any members of committee or other teams will not be tolerated.
  • Misbehavior or arguing with the Judges will result in disqualification of team.
  • Teams can choose any form of dance and music for their performance (following the theme is not compulsory).
  • Teams are requested to bring their music tracks in at least three different devices to avoid technical disturbances, Ex: - Pen drives, mobile phones (with proper required cables/cords), etc.
  • Entry inside the college premises will not be allowed without respective valid college/faculty ID cards.
  • It is compulsory for team members along with their helpers to wear their college IDs at all time when inside college premises.
  • Disregard of any rule will not be tolerated and will lead to negative marking and maybe even disqualification.
  • Decisions of the Judges will be final and binding.
  • Registration Fees will not be returned under any circumstances.
  • Teams must mail the list of props that they are going to use during performance, at destival@outlook.com, five days prior to the event.
  • In case of any unavoidable situation, the decision of the judge & the Organizing Committee will be final and binding.
  • Props are allowed but no additional marks will be given for their usage.
  • Exceeding time limit will lead to negative marking.
  • All the participating teams must take care of their belongings, the Organizing team will not be held responsible, if anything goes missing.
  • A team won’t be allowed to view the performance of the other groups.
Rules for using Props:
  • Props which can dirty or damage the stage will not be allowed. Ex: - water, colored powder, glitter, confetti, sparks, props involving fire, etc.
  • Sharp objects or any object that require professional attention are not allowed.
  • Use of any such props will result in negative marking and might also result in disqualification.
  • Teams must mail the list of props that they are going to use during performance, at destival@outlook.com, five days prior to the event.
Judging Criteria:
  • Choreography
  • Creativity
  • Sense of space
  • Facial expressions
  • Synchronization and group formation
  • Over-all Performance

Performance
  • 1. A stage time of 25 minutes will be allotted to each band for the performance including the sound check. (Time will start from the moment the band steps on the stage).
  • 2. A minimum of 3 band members and a maximum of 8 band members should be present.
  • 3. Exceeding the time limit will leave negative marking to the hands of the judges and sound will be cut off after 27 minutes of stage time.
  • 4. It is important that a band plays at least one original composition.
  • 5. Use of backing tracks (with vocals or drums) and looping is not allowed. (Use of samples is allowed. For more details regarding this, please contact the coordinators).
Judgement Criteria and Miscellaneous
  • 1. Acts will be judged based on tightness, tones, instrumental proficiency, vocal proficiency, stage presence, impact and ability to master front of the house sound.
  • 2. The judge’s decision is final and binding.
  • 3. The selected bands must carry the selection acknowledgement, college ID and governmental proof of ID and the payment receipt along with them on the day of the event.
  • 4. Bands coming in from outside Jaipur may opt for accommodation in the hostel premises.
  • 5. Registrations for the final selected bands will be done online for an amount of INR 1500 per band. (Registration fee is non- refundable under all circumstances, including withdrawal from the competition.)
  • 6. Any distasteful behaviour, use of derogatory language and/or damage to the stage and any MUJ property will result in an automatic disqualification at the hands of management and judges.
  • 7. The band is responsible for their own belongings. In case of any loss or damage to the band’s gear/equipment, neither the college nor the event organisers will be held responsible for the same.
  • 8. MUJ will not be held responsible for any broken equipment, injuries, or any other negative occurrences outside of staff’s control.
  • 9. Consumption of Alcohol or any other illicit substance is strictly prohibited and may lead to disqualification.
